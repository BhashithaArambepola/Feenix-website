Data Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as:

  • Name: For identifying you as a customer.
  • Email Address: For communication and sending updates related to services.
  • Phone Number: For customer support or notifications.
  • Payment Information: For billing purposes related to our services.
  • IP Address: For security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data may be used for the following purposes:

  • To process orders or requests related to our services.
  • To communicate important information, such as updates or changes to services.
  • To respond to your inquiries or feedback.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Protection

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Your personal data is stored securely and only accessible by authorized personnel.

4. Sharing of Personal Information

We do not share or sell your personal information to third parties except for the following:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers for purposes such as processing payments or hosting services, but only to the extent necessary to fulfill these services.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose personal data in response to legal requests from governmental or regulatory authorities.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience. These tools allow us to analyze website usage and improve our services.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of any inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@feenixmarketing.com.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Data Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
